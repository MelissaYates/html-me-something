Oui, Oui Madame!

Let's get cooking!

“Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food” hearkens back to Hippocrates,
the father of medicine. This quote, though thousands of years old, acknowledges
the importance of healthy eating and how the nutrients in various foods have healing
properties. A healthy lifestyle with good nutrition is vital for maintaining
good health and disease prevention.

Does this mean that life will no longer be fun and that you will not get to enjoy the tastes you truly enjoy? I am here to tell you that this is not the case.
Come along with me on this journey! You never know, you just might end up liking it!

To keep us running well, we need to choose our foods wisely and keep our diet well balanced with the four main food groups:

  1. Fruits
  2. Grains
  3. Nuts and Seeds
  4. Vegetables
